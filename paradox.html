From Chaos to Clarity

Implementing "Operating Procedures" as we grow

The Biological Limit

  • Brooks's Law: Adding people to a chaotic process just creates more chaos and slows down delivery.
  • Dunbar's Number: Humans can only maintain ~150 relationships. Beyond that, trust requires structure, not just proximity.
  • The Solution: Procedures act as an API for Humans. They replace "finding out who to ask" with "knowing where to look."

The Ask:

We need to shift from "Tribal Knowledge" (asking around) to "Explicit Knowledge" (documented processes).

This isn't bureaucracy; it is the infrastructure required to scale past 50 employees without grinding to a halt.
